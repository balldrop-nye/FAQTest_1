 About BallDrop.com & NYE.com

We sell tickets to New Year's Eve parties in Times Square with guaranteed views of the ball drop directly from the venue, as well as tickets to parties in the Times Square viewing area that have a history of letting guests exit to see the ball drop and then re-enter the party. We also sell passes that give you access to parties at multiple venues in Times Square, which is fun because it lets you move around the area and to various parties. And we sell tickets to other fun New Yera's Eve parties that are not in the Times Square area, including a number of cruises. We have parties that are for adults age 21 and up, and parties that welcome all ages.

 About the Times Square New Year's Eve Ball Drop Celebrations

To come to Times Square to see the ball drop is free and does not require tickets, but you may need to arrive extremely early (sometimes as early as the night before) and be willing to stand out in the cold for many hours without access to bathrooms or seating. Alternatively, we sell tickets for parties in Times Square that will allow you to enter the Times Square area much later (as late as 8pm on NYE) and give you access to area parties that will have food, drinks, and music leading up to the ball drop at midnight.

The Times Square New Year's Eve Ball descends from a flagpole at the top of One Times Square, which is located at 7th Ave, between 42nd St & 43rd St. It can be seen along Broadway, from 41st St to 50th St, and along 7th Ave, as far north as 59th St.

 Street Closures

The New York Police Department will be closing down access to Times Square from 40th St to 59th St & from 6th Ave to 8th Ave. The exact times that these blocks close will depend on when people begin arriving, which is normally the night before, and the area is usually closed off entirely to non ticket holders by around noon on NYE.

Ticket Types

Here is info about our different ticket types:

Seated VIP is available for singles or any size group, and gives you and your party seating together at a shared area with other guests. There is no bottle service with Seated VIP but you would still have access to the open bar included in the ticket price.

Ultra VIP and Platinum VIP type tickets are available for purchase for groups of 4 or more people. These tickets will give you seating reserved for your group and includes bottle service in the ticket price. The number of bottles you will be provided is determined by your ticket type and group size.

Couples tickets provide seating and bottle service for two, and the cost is per-couple not per-person.

General Admission and Access tickets are standing room only and do not allow for seating.

(*Addional ticket types will be available for certain events. **Please look closely at all advertised details before purchase)
